# Critical Rational Personality Studies

He discerned that both of these discourses heavily rely on insecure methods of study.

* Marxism relies on Historicism which was popularly represented as a dialectic by *George Hegel*, which is a type of philosophical Holism, where meaning cannot be understood by reducing 
* Psychoanalysis relies on Introspection
